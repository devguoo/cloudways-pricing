# Code of Conduct

This repository is maintained as a practical Cloudways pricing and billing reference.

Participants should:

- Be respectful and factual.
- Prefer source-backed corrections.
- Avoid spam, affiliate link drops, and misleading official-sounding claims.
- Avoid sharing private account, payment, or support information.

Maintainers may remove issues, pull requests, or comments that are abusive, promotional, misleading, or unsafe.
